---

## SRS Stage Intervals

| Stage | Review After | Advance When |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| 1 | 1 day | Recalled approach + key insight without help |
| 2 | 3 days | Recalled approach + key insight without help |
| 3 | 7 days | Recalled approach + key insight without help |
| Graduated | 14 days | Consistently recalled. Move to passive review. |

## Review Ratings

- **Good:** Recalled the approach and key insight. Advance to next stage.
- **Hard:** Recalled partially. Stay at current stage. Review again at same interval.
- **Again:** Couldn't recall. Reset to Stage 1.
